About Mumbai

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is the financial capital of India. Located on the western coast, it is a vibrant city known for its iconic landmarks, diverse culture, and bustling energy. From the Gateway of India to Marine Drive, Mumbai offers something for everyone.

Top Places to Visit

  • Gateway of India: An iconic monument overlooking the Arabian Sea.
  • Marine Drive: A picturesque promenade perfect for sunsets.
  • Elephanta Caves: Ancient rock-cut caves with exquisite carvings.
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus: A UNESCO World Heritage site and historic railway station.
  • Juhu Beach: A popular spot for relaxation and local street food.

Mumbai's Culture

Mumbai is a melting pot of cultures, blending traditional Marathi heritage with influences from all over India and beyond. The city is famous for its Bollywood film industry, street food, festivals, and a never-ending spirit of resilience and diversity.
