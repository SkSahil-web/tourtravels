About Kolkata

Kolkata, formerly known as Calcutta, is the cultural capital of India. With its rich history, art, and vibrant traditions, Kolkata is a city that never fails to enchant its visitors.

Food Culture

Kolkata is famous for its street food, sweets like rasgulla, and delicious fish curries. Don't forget to try the famous Kathi Rolls and Puchkas (Pani Puri).

Beautiful Places to Visit in Kolkata

Victoria Memorial

An iconic landmark, reflecting British architecture and Kolkata's history.

Howrah Bridge

A symbol of Kolkata, this engineering marvel connects two parts of the city.

Eco Park

A sprawling urban park offering green landscapes, boating, and more.

Alipore Zoo

Alipore Zoological Garden, Kolkata, India's oldest formally stated zoological park.

Indian Museum

India's oldest museum, showcasing art, history, and culture.
